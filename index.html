Hello, I'm Shalom

Hi, I'm Shalom Chidi-Azuwike, a 20-year-old Software Engineering student at the Federal University of Technology Owerri, Nigeria. I'm passionate about AI, Data Science, Web3, and storytelling. As a Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador and Samsung Student Ambassador, I advocate for digital skills and innovation. My love for reading, sparked at age four, led me to author books like The Billionaire's Son and Caught in Between under the pseudonym Jaime Cabary. I've developed websites like KeedCeeJaye, Phantomzcollectionx.vercel.app, and my portfolio, plus apps like Bizvest, ScriptureDash, and a GPS app. My projects include Janessa AI, MarsoVerse, and a Weather app. Inspired by Elon Musk, I aim to create robotic art and contribute to humanity's multiplanetary future.
